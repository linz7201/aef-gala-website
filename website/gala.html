About the Gala

The AEF Gala is a night of celebration, featuring food, drinks, music, and silent & live auctions, all at the Hilton Hotel located in San Gabriel. It is held annually by the Alhambra Education Foundation, the fundraising partner for the Alhambra Unified School District.

The Gala's Impact

At the AEF Gala, we recognize and honor those who give back to our community

…and we’ve been doing just that since 2005!

Over these 17 years, the Gala has raised over $750,000 to improve the lives of the children in our schools. It has funded music classes, counselors, supplies, computers, internet access, books, uniforms, programs, and so much more.

Your support has made this possible.
